How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Tile exchange: Every 2000 score gains one more chance (EX) to exchange two tiles. Click on EX, and click 2 tiles in order to complete exchange. When in EX mode, tile moves are disabled, but click EX once more will return to normal mode. The first tile selected could be canceled by another click on the same tile. 4096 score is rather easy with EX functionality. Enjoy?

Note: This site is the official version of 2048. You can play it on your phone via http://git.io/2048. All other apps or sites are derivatives or fakes, and should be used with caution.
